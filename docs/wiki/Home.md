# Job Portal Management System Wiki

Welcome to the **Job Portal Management System** wiki.

This wiki is the central place for project planning, implementation notes, deployment steps, and maintenance documentation.

## Project Overview

The Job Portal Management System is a Laravel-based platform that supports:

- Public job browsing and filtering
- Employer job posting and management
- Admin moderation and management dashboard
- Category, location, and role management
- Multiple apply methods (WhatsApp, Email, External URL)

## Current Status

- Admin panel: completed
- Responsive forms: completed
- README/documentation: completed
- Production hardening checklist: in progress
